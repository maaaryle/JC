Crushing

Summit Civils LP provides crushing and screening services to produce a wide variety of materials including engineered products with 100% fracture to custom products to help our private resource clients best utilize their material sources and meet aggregate needs. We have in-house expertise to achieve the best possible yield products from alluvial and hard rock sources using the highest of standards for our quality control and testing.

We have a modern fleet of portable plants with various configurations which enable us to deal with a variety of situations efficiently. Our crushing and screening equipment include Jaws, Cones, Impactors, Crushers, Multi-Deck Screens, and Stockpiling Conveyors and Radial Stackers. We utilize the Aggflow software to match our crushing equipment with the material source to obtain minimal reject and maximize usable product.

Our knowledgeable management has the expertise to run a successful crushing operation. They understand the key is to put the Health and Safety of employees first; provide necessary training to ensure that equipment operations hazards, dust and noise awareness issues are all recognized. Each site is treated separate and has its own individual site specific safety and work plans. We invite all of our clients to review and comment on these plans.
