Culverts Multi-Plate

Water management is typical for every location whether a permanent conveyance is needed for a stream across a road or part of a greater storm water treatment facility; Summit Civils is capable of installing culverts of various sizes and materials. A wide variety of wall thicknesses (gages), corrugations, diameters and pipe section lengths permit a choice of materials to meet specific job site requirements. Deep fill culverts are particular challenges that our experience provides for success time and time again.

Summit Civils is able to deliver multi‐plate structures efficiently to meet your needs from large diameter fish passage culverts, to Arch Bridges capable of bearing 150 Metric Ton haul units. Summit Civils favors arch bridges for stream crossing to minimize the impact to existing stream bed as a final product as well as during the construction phase, and allows for a substantially higher flow rates compared to typical culverts. Additionally multi‐plate structures allow for expedited delivery and logistics for remote locations. Feasibility of multi‐plate structures is justified up 15 meters in width before analyzing the need to install a bridge. Multi‐plate structures eliminate the need to ship large and costly girders.

Upfront planning, risk assessment and project works plans outline the appropriate means and methods to ensure the safety of our workers and successful completion of the work scope.
